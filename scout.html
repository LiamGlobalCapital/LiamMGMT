Tips for getting scouted

Professional model scouts are looking for something different than you might expect. We don't want to see you in your fanciest outfit with lots of makeup. We want to see you at your most natural.

  • Have a clean face with no makeup.
  • Pull your hair back from your face.
  • Wear a form-fitting outfit so we can see your shape.
  • Use natural daylight whenever possible.
  • Plain, uncluttered background.
Recruitment warning

Please be aware that there are individuals who falsely claim to be representatives of Liam Mgmt.

  • You should never pay to attend a casting or be represented.
  • All Liam Mgmt correspondence comes from email addresses ending in @liammgmt.com.
  • If anyone contacts you claiming to represent us, verify by emailing desk@liammgmt.com directly before responding.
